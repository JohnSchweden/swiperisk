## Analysis

| Version | Strength | Weakness |
|---|---|---|
| Explanatory | Clear for cold visitors; answers "what is this"; includes duration | Loses voice; too earnest; feels like marketing copy |
| Tight Deadpan | Keeps K-Maru's voice consistent; tighter; funnier | Assumes more context; less explicit about "10 minutes" and "no signup" |

**Decision**: Implement Tight Deadpan. Voice is load-bearing for this product.
